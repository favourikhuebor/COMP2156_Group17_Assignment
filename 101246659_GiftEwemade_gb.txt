George Brown College is a public college in Toronto, Canada.
The college has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.
Popular programs include business, technology, hospitality, and health sciences.
The college emphasizes hands-on learning through co-op programs and industry partnerships.
Support services include career counseling, tutoring, and mental health support.
George Brown College has a large international student community from over 100 countries.
George Brown College offers various clubs, events, and extracurricular activities for students.
The college collaborates with top companies to provide co-op and internship opportunities.
George Brown College offers online and hybrid courses for flexible learning.
