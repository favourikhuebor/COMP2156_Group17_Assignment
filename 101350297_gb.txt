George Brown College is a public applied arts and technology college located in Toronto, Canada. 
Founded in 1967, it serves thousands of domestic and international students across various disciplines.

Campuses:
1. Casa Loma Campus - Focuses on technology and engineering programs.
2. St. James Campus - Home to business, culinary arts, and media programs.
3. Waterfront Campus - Specializes in health sciences programs.

George Brown College is known for its strong industry connections and hands-on learning approach, ensuring that students gain practical experience before entering the workforce.
